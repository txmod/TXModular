NHHall St


Module type: Audio Insert (stereo input, stereo output)


Introduction:

A general-purpose stereo-to-stereo reverb. Random modulation adds lush chorus effects to the sound.


Controls:


Main Button


Pre-delay - delay before reverb starts.

Reverb time

Infinite hold - when on, reverb time becomes infinite.

Stereo blend - Rate at which audio spreads between the left and right channels. At 0, the two channel paths are kept separate. At 1, they blend together nearly instantly. In between, the stereo spread happens slowly over time.

Dry-Wet Mix - this sets the mix of the original signal with the affected signal.


Character Button


Freq range - the range of frequencies that will be used.

Low ratio - The decay time multiplier for low frequencies. Set this to 1 to use the same decay time as the mid frequencies.

High ratio - The decay time multiplier for high frequencies. Set this to 1 to use the same decay time as the mid frequencies.

Early diffusion - most normal reverbs will use 0.5 to 0.9. Higher values have a sharper attack and add metallic coloration to the signal.

Late diffusion - most normal reverbs will use 0.5 to 0.9. Higher values have a sharper attack and add metallic coloration to the signal.

Mod depth - Modulation depth for late reflections.

Mod rate - Modulation rate for late reflections.
