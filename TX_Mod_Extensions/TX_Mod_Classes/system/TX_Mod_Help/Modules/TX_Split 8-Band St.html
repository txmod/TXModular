Split 8-Band St


Module type: Audio Source (stereo input & 8 stereo outputs)


Introduction:

This module splits a stereo audio input into 8 stereo frequency bands.

Once a module has been created, it will appear in the list of possible destinations for audio channels, so you can send an audio signal to it.


Note: Split frequencies cannot be modulated or automated.


Controls:


Split Freq 1 - sets the frequency to split bands 1 & 2.

Split Freq 2 - sets the frequency to split bands 2 & 3.

Split Freq 3 - sets the frequency to split bands 3 & 4.

Split Freq 4 - sets the frequency to split bands 4 & 5.

Split Freq 5 - sets the frequency to split bands 5 & 6.

Split Freq 6 - sets the frequency to split bands 6 & 7.

Split Freq 7 - sets the frequency to split bands 7 & 8.

Output level - sets the overall output level.
