Normalizer St


Module type: Audio Insert (stereo input, stereo output)


Introduction:

This module flattens the dynamics (i.e. the volume or amplitude) of a stereo audio signal to a certain level.


Controls:

Lookahead - the time which the normalizer takes to process the audio. This also imposes a delay on the output signal.

(Technical note: Shorter times will produce shorter delays, but may introduce amplitude modulation artifacts.)

In Gain - this can be used to boost or cut the volume of the audio signal before limiting.

Level - the output level which the signal is normalized to.

Dry-Wet Mix - this sets the mix of the original signal with the affected signal. For send effects this will usually be set to 1 - i.e. 100% affected signal.
