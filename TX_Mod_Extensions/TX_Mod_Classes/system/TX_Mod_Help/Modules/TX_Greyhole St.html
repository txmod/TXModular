Greyhole St


Module type: Audio Insert (stereo input, stereo output)


Introduction:

From the SuperCollider help: “A complex echo-like effect … a diffuser (like a mini-reverb) connected in a feedback system with a long modulated delay-line. Excels at producing spacey washes of sound.”


Controls:

Reverb Time

Size - scales size of delay-lines within the diffusion unit, producing the impression of a larger or smaller space. Values below 1 can sound metallic.

Damping - damping of high-frequencies as the delay decays. 0 is no damping, 1 is very strong damping

Shape - controls pattern of echoes produced by the diffuser. At very low values, the diffuser acts like a delay-line whose length is controlled by the 'size' parameter. Medium values produce a slow build-up of echoes, giving the sound a reversed-like quality. Values of 0.707 or greater than produce smooth exponentially decaying echoes.

Feedback - amount of feedback through the system. Sets the number of repeating echoes. A setting of 1.0 produces infinite sustain.

Modulation Depth - depth of delay-line modulation. Use in combination with Modulation Freq to produce chorus and pitch-variations in the echoes.

Dry-Wet Mix - this sets the mix of the original signal with the affected signal. For send effects this will usually be set to 1 - i.e. 100% affected signal.
